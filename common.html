📖 About NotyCaption Pro

NotyCaption Pro is an AI-powered smart caption generator that uses OpenAI's Whisper model to generate accurate, perfectly synced subtitles for your audio and video files.

✨ Features

  • 🎯 AI-Powered Transcription - OpenAI Whisper for high accuracy
  • 🎤 Professional Vocal Separation - Remove background music
  • 🌍 Multi-Language Support - 17+ languages + Auto Detect
  • 🔄 Translation Mode - Transcribe and translate to English
  • 📝 Smart Subtitle Formatting - SRT/ASS with word-level timing
  • ⚡ GPU Acceleration - Google Colab for fast processing

🔒 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Data We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ Your audio files (they stay in your Google Drive)
  • ❌ Your generated subtitles/captions
  • ❌ Your personal identifiable information
  • ❌ Your IP address

Data We Store

  • 📦 Google OAuth tokens (encrypted, in your browser)
  • 🍪 User preferences (language, words per line, output format)

Third-Party Services

  • Google Drive API - For file storage
  • Google Colab - For AI processing
  • Google OAuth - For authentication

Your files never pass through our servers. Everything happens between your browser, Google Drive, and Google Colab.
